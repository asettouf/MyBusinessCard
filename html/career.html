My Career

With the engineer mindset I made my own during my education, I built my professional career with this idea of understanding how computers work, what happens from the creation of a packet in the kernel, to it reaching another computer, and finally how is it analysed and for example rendered, in the case of a browser. My first step was at Gemalto where I was a system administrator on Linux CentOs, and I discover the joy of scripting and modifying an environment to both satisfy what users expect of the system and to enhance it. My second step was working in the web development with CGI for Louis Vuitton, which was a logical step since I learnt a lot at school that year about the J2E framework, which is the backend of a Java Web Application in general. I learnt many techniques regarding the new HTML5 technologies, under the supervision of Thibaut de Broca, my team lead, who always pushed us to go harder. Finally, I wanted to see a little more about the world we live in, as well as discover what it was like to administer windows environment, and Lexmark hungary gave me that possibility, and after a successful collaboration with them, I am more than eager to go back to programming today.
